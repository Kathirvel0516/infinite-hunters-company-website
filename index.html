NAPS Apprenticeship Programme

The National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) was launched by the Government of India in 2016 to boost skilled apprenticeships in the service sector. Employers receive 25% of the stipend reimbursed, up to ₹1,500/month per apprentice. We handle everything.

  • Candidate sourcing — we identify ITI, diploma and graduate candidates for your trade requirements
  • BOAT/MHRD portal registration — all documentation and portal submissions handled by our team
  • Basic Training (BT) delivery — structured 200+ hour BT through empanelled institutes
  • Stipend management — monthly calculation, disbursement, and government claim reimbursement
  • Document compliance — log books, attendance registers, and audit submissions maintained
  • Government liaison — direct communication with Apprenticeship Advisers on your behalf
  • Cost reduction — significantly lower manpower cost vs regular employees with full skill benefit

Statutory Compliance Services

Non-compliance with labour laws can result in heavy penalties and serious legal exposure. Our dedicated compliance team manages all your statutory obligations so your business stays fully protected and audit-ready at all times — across all states of operation.

  • Factory & Shops Act registration — new registrations and renewals handled state-wise
  • Labour registers maintenance — Form A, B, C, D, muster rolls, wage registers — all maintained
  • Contract Labour (R&A) Act — principal employer and contractor compliance managed
  • Annual return filings — all required annual returns filed before due dates, no penalties
  • Minimum wages advisory — state-wise minimum wage updates and implementation support
  • Audit & inspection support — we represent and assist you during Labour Department inspections
  • Multi-state compliance — single-point management of compliance obligations across all your states
